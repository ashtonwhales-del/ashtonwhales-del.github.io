🎣 HookBook: Fishing Privacy Policy Last updated: June 2026 HookBook: Fishing ("HookBook", "we", "us") is a free fishing diary app. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it. Data Stored on Your Device HookBook stores the following data locally on your device only. This data is never transmitted to our servers: Catch records (species, weight, length, date, notes) Catch photos (stored as compressed images on your device) Saved fishing spots (name, coordinates, notes) Your username and profile photo App preferences (dark mode, etc.) Deleting the app removes all locally stored data permanently. Location Data HookBook may request your location to: Auto-tag where you caught a fish Show nearby public fishing spots on the map Your location is used only within the app and is never transmitted to us or third parties. You can deny location permission and still use most app features. Camera and Photos HookBook requests camera and photo library access only to let you photograph your catches. Photos are stored locally on your device only. Advertising HookBook uses Google AdMob to display fishing-related ads. AdMob may use your device's advertising identifier (IDFA) to show relevant ads. You will be asked for permission before this identifier is accessed. You can manage this in your iPhone's Privacy & Security settings under Tracking. AdMob's privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy Third-Party Services HookBook uses these third-party services to provide features: Google AdMob — advertising OpenStreetMap / Overpass API — public fishing spot data (no personal data sent) iNaturalist API — fish species observation data (no personal data sent) Recreation.gov API — public recreation area data (no personal data sent) Open-Meteo API — weather data for catch tagging (coordinates only, no personal data) When you use the Fish ID feature, HookBook opens ChatGPT or Gemini in your browser. Any photos you share with those services are subject to their own privacy policies. Data We Do Not Collect HookBook does not collect, store, or transmit: Your name or email address Your catch history or fishing locations to our servers Any health or financial information Any data for sale to third parties Children's Privacy HookBook is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. Changes to This Policy We may update this policy. Continued use of HookBook after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
